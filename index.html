The Rules

Character Creation
All character creation choices must match your decree exactly — race, gender, and class. The addon will reject the import if any of these do not match the character you are logging in on.
Self-Found Mode
Self-Found must be enabled during character creation and cannot be toggled off. It restricts player-to-player economy only. You may not use the Auction House, trade with other players, or receive items through the in-game mail from other players. Vendor purchases, quest rewards, world drops, and items crafted by yourself are all permitted.
Challenges
Ironman Addon Enforced
  • No talent points — ever
  • White or grey quality gear only. No greens, blues, or epics regardless of source — quest rewards included
  • No enchants of any kind
  • No grouping with other players at any time
  • Cannot be stacked with any other challenge
Self-Sufficient Addon Enforced
  • Only gear you crafted yourself or found as a drop or quest reward
  • No Auction House purchases
  • No gear bought from vendors
  • Vendor food and water are permitted to be purchased from vendors — no consumables
Teetotaler Addon Enforced
  • No consumable buffs of any kind may be used at any point during your run
  • Banned: elixirs, flasks, scrolls, jujus, food buffs (Well Fed), healing potions, mana potions, protection potions, weapon oils and stones
  • Permitted: bandages, Warlock Healthstones, Mage conjured food and water, Rogue poisons, vendor food and water for out-of-combat regen, class self-buffs
  • Cannot be stacked with any other challenge
No Hearthstone Addon Enforced
  • Your Hearthstone must be destroyed immediately upon run start
  • The Unstuck service is also banned
  • Travel everywhere on foot or by flight path
No Mounts Addon Enforced
  • No mounts of any kind, ever
  • Class mounts (Paladin, Warlock) are banned under this challenge
Face Your Death Addon Enforced
  • Bans consumable immunity effects not sourced from your class or talents
  • Flask of Petrification (item 13506) is banned — grants complete invulnerability
  • Light of Elune (item 5816) is banned — grants temporary immunity to damage
  • Limited Invulnerability Potion (item 3387) is banned — grants temporary immunity to physical damage
  • Possession triggers a warning; using the buff triggers permanent run failure
No Professions Addon Enforced
  • No primary or secondary professions may be learned for the duration of your run
  • All eleven banned: Herbalism, Mining, Skinning, Blacksmithing, Tailoring, Leatherworking, Enchanting, Alchemy, First Aid, Cooking, Fishing
  • Rogue Lockpicking is a class mechanic, not a profession — it is unaffected
  • Cannot roll alongside the Kamisayo Speedrun Build
No World Buffs Addon Enforced
  • No world buffs may be active when entering combat
  • Banned buffs include: Rallying Cry of the Dragonslayer, Warchief's Blessing, Songflower Serenade, Spirit of Zandalar, Sayge's Fortune, DMT buffs, Traces of Silithyst, and others
  • Involuntary application is handled with a 10-second grace window — remove the buff before entering combat to avoid a run failure
  • Entering combat with an active world buff after the grace window expires triggers permanent run failure
Special Builds

  • Following Kamisayo's RestedXP leveling guide is recommended but not required

  • No profession restrictions
Builds
You are bound to the talent path assigned by your decree. The addon monitors your talent distribution at higher levels. Checks occur on login and on level-up, comparing your current distribution against the expected allocation for the previous level's phase threshold — accounting for the fact that you may not have assigned a point yet or may not have respecced since crossing into a new phase.
Your build is considered compliant if your talent points are distributed proportionally to the expected tree allocation for your assigned phase, within a generous tolerance. Three violations across the entire run results in a permanent run failure. Alt builds follow the same validation rules as standard builds.
Professions
You must have both assigned professions selected by level 13. Checks begin at level 14 and occur on every login and level-up thereafter. Both profession slots must be filled from your assigned combination. Secondary professions (Cooking, Fishing, First Aid) are always permitted and never checked.
Exceptions
  • Mining + Engineering: Skinning is permitted as a bonus third profession. You must always hold Mining + Engineering. A 5-minute grace window applies when dropping to swap
  • Kamisayo: Any two of Mining, Blacksmithing, Herbalism, or Alchemy are valid. You must always hold two valid professions from this pool. A 5-minute grace window applies when dropping to swap
  • Dropping a profession outside these exceptions is an instant run failure
Dungeons
Your decree assigns a list of dungeons drawn from your faction's available pool. The purpose of this list is to vary your run with each attempt — forcing you into level ranges and zones you might otherwise skip, and requiring you to compensate through questing in areas you wouldn't normally prioritize when a dungeon falls outside your assigned list.
You are required to complete every assigned dungeon. Completion means killing the final boss while they still grant experience — the kill must occur before the boss goes grey to your character. Scarlet Monastery counts as four separate completions (one per wing). Maraudon counts as three (one per wing).
Entering any dungeon not on your assigned list and participating in any kill — including trash — triggers an immediate permanent run failure. The addon monitors this at all times.

FAQs

The Basics
What is this?
The Scroll of Binding is a randomizer for Classic WoW Anniversary Hardcore. It rolls your race, class, build, professions, and optional challenges, then gives you an import code for the companion addon to track your run.
What's the goal?
To bring a roguelike feel to Hardcore WoW. Every run is different — randomized constraints, permadeath stakes, no safety net. The scroll decides who you are. Your job is to survive it.
Do I have to follow every result?
Yes. The scroll is binding. That's the point. If you don't want to play what it gives you, reroll — but once you've imported the code into the addon and earned XP, your fate is sealed.
When do I import the code?
Immediately after character creation, before earning any experience. The addon will refuse the import once you have XP.
Builds
What's a Standard vs Alt build?
Standard builds are common — well-documented, viable specs for leveling. Alt builds are rare — unconventional paths that work, but will test you. Rolling an alt build triggers a special moment and affects what other challenges can appear.
What are Special builds?
A small number of classes have a rare chance at a Special build — a curated challenge run with its own fixed rules and restrictions baked in. Special builds suppress challenge rolls entirely and may lock professions or Self-Found mode depending on the build.
Are builds enforced?
Yes. The addon checks your talent distribution at login once you're high enough level. Repeated violations permanently end the run.
Self-Found Mode
What does Self-Found mean?
No Auction House. No trading with other players. No mail from other players. Every piece of gear must be found by you — drops, quest rewards, or crafted by yourself.
Can I use the vendor?
Yes. Vendors are fine. Self-Found restricts player-to-player economy only.
Challenges
How likely am I to get a challenge?
Challenges are uncommon. A second challenge stacked on top is rare. The scroll handles all compatibility automatically — some combinations simply can't appear together.
What are the challenges?
Ironman — No talents, white or grey gear only, no enchants, no grouping. Cannot be stacked with any other challenge.
Teetotaler — No consumable buffs of any kind. No elixirs, flasks, scrolls, potions, food buffs, or weapon oils. Cannot be stacked with any other challenge.
Self-Sufficient — Only gear you crafted or personally found. No AH, no vendor gear.
No Professions — No primary or secondary professions may be learned. Cannot roll with the Kamisayo build.
No World Buffs — No world buffs active when entering combat. A 10-second grace window applies to involuntary application.
No Hearthstone — No Hearthstone, no Unstuck. Walk everywhere.
Face Your Death — No consumable immunity items. Flask of Petrification, Light of Elune, and Limited Invulnerability Potion are banned.
No Mounts — No mounts of any kind, ever. Class mounts included.
Are challenges enforced?
All challenges are tracked by the addon. Ironman, Teetotaler, No Professions, No World Buffs, Face Your Death, and No Mounts are enforced automatically. Self-Sufficient, No Hearthstone, and No Mounts enforcement is partially automated — the addon monitors where it can and flags violations; some edge cases are accepted as undetectable.
Dungeons
How are dungeons assigned?
The scroll assigns a randomized list drawn from your faction's available pool. Faction matters — some dungeons are off-limits entirely, and others are weighted based on whether they're in your faction's territory.
What counts as completing a dungeon?
Killing the final boss. Scarlet Monastery and Maraudon each count as multiple completions — one per wing.
Can I enter dungeons not on my list?
No. Any kill inside a dungeon not on your list — including trash — triggers an immediate permanent run failure. The addon monitors this at all times.
The Addon
What does the addon do?
It tracks your entire run: talent compliance, dungeon completions, challenge confirmations, and violation monitoring. Your current status is always accessible via the minimap button or slash command. Run failures and level 60 completions are recorded permanently.
What happens if my run fails?
The addon marks the run as permanently failed and records the violation. The summary is still viewable, but monitoring stops — the run is over. On Hardcore, you're likely dead anyway.
